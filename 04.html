Open Evenings

Come along to one of our open evenings to meet the current cat residents, chat with our volunteers, find out more about what we do and why we need your help to keep the charity running!

If you're thinking about rehoming a cat, our open evenings are the perfect opportunity to drop by and find out if you're suitable for rehoming one of our cats.

We're sure that you'll fall in love with at least one of the current residents, but if not we can take your details and contact you if a more suitable cat moves into the centre.

Summer BBQ and Open Day

Our annual summer BBQ and open day is our opportunity to thank our volunteers for all their hard work and give a little bit back to the local community.

Come by for some delicious BBQ food, including locally sourced meat and vegetables, and perhaps a pint of our delicious homemade cider or two.

The event is suitable for children and there will be face painters, a bouncy castle and other crafty stalls to keep them entertained - as well as playing with the cats of course!

There will be a raffle with lots of amazing prizes to be won. We'll also have our infamous bread and cakes stall to make sure you are nicely full up.

FAQs

Find answers to common questions about participating in our events.

To register for our events, simply visit our website and fill out the registration form. Make sure to provide all the required information.
The requirements for participating in our events may vary. Please check the event details for specific requirements such as age restrictions or special skills.
We have certain policies in place to ensure the safety and enjoyment of all participants. These policies may include rules regarding behavior, dress code, or prohibited items. Please review the event policies before attending.
Whether pets are allowed at our events depends on the specific event and venue. Please check the event details for information on whether pets are permitted.
Some of our events may have an entry fee, while others may be free to attend. Please refer to the event details for information on any applicable fees.
